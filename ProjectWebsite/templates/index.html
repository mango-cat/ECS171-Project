Types of Urls

Phishing:
Points to a website that mimics legitimate websites or services, tricking users into thinking they are interacting with a trusted entity.
Malware:
Associated with websites or web pages that host or distribute malicious software, commonly known as malware.
Defacement:
Involves altering the appearance or content of a website without the owner's permission, often with the intention of making a political, social, or personal statement.
Benign:
Points to legitimate websites that are safe and free from security risks.
